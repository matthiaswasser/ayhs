American Yawp for High School

Stanford's American Yawp is my favorite textbook - open-source, clear, and actually provides citations - but it's a bit above the reading level of most (not all) kids in high school, which is where I teach. This is an attempt to rewrite it with:

  • an adjustable reading level, and
  • embedded flashcards via Andy Matsuchak's Orbit
